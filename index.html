About Us

Established in 2021, Adept Global is a company designed to help CREATIVES have a legal and professional approach to their craft, operating in the Entertainment, Arts, Film, and sports sectors. We believe that the creative is a professional and should be seen and treated as such.

Our Vision

To see the majority of African Creatives making a living from their Craft

Our Mission

  • Educate and empower the creative to see the significance of their unique abilities to the world
  • To help the creative protect their intellectual property and copyrights.
  • To render services that give the creative a professional outlook.
  • To help the creative build the right systems and structure to their craft.
